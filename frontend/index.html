About

Rediscover Sanctuary Cap Cana, the Dominican Republic's most exclusive adults-only all-inclusive resort. You'll be delighted by the resorts $45 million modernization as you explore spectacular suites, artfully crafted lobbies, updated restaurants and bars, enhanced world-class spa and state-of-the-art fitness center. Sanctuary Cap Cana, A Luxury Collection Adult All-Inclusive Resort is located in Cap Cana, a private gated community retreat inside Punta Cana. Cap Cana is made up of 30,000 acres of flawless beaches. The resort features 323 luxury suites, each with a private balcony or terrace overlooking the stunning Caribbean Sea. Guests enjoy world-class dining at 10 restaurants, unlimited premium beverages, and access to an award-winning spa. The pristine beach stretches over 1.5 miles of white sand, offering water sports, snorkeling, and kayaking.
